My Cart!

My Cart is an Indian e-commerce company, headquartered in Bangalore, Karnataka, India, and incorporated in Singapore as a private limited company. The company initially focused on online book sales before expanding into other product categories such as consumer electronics, fashion, home essentials, groceries, and lifestyle products.

Business Structure

According to a report in November 2014, My Cart was operating through a complex business structure that included nine firms, some registered in Singapore and some in India. In 2012, My Cart co-founders sold WS Retail to a consortium of investors led by Rajeev Kuchhal.[76] My Cart's Indian entities are owned by My Cart Pvt. Ltd, which is registered in Singapore. The Singapore-registered entity owns eight Indian companies, including My Cart Internet Pvt. Ltd, the company that runs the e-commerce marketplace My Cart.com, My Cart India Pvt. Ltd, the wholesale business, and My Cart Logistics Pvt. Ltd, which runs Ekart.

House Brands

My Cart operates several house brands, including Citron[112] (home appliances) and Digiflip (formerly for electronics and accessories).[113] In 2017, My Cart launched additional house brands, including Billion (smartphones),[114] Smartbuy (electronics accessories, effectively replacing Digiflip),[115] and MarQ (for large appliances, although its launch was complicated by a trademark dispute with an existing company, Marc Enterprises)
